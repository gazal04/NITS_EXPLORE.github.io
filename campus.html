Hostel Culture and Allocation

The Institute has got nine Boys hostels and three Girls hostels. All the hostels have their Mess Management Committee to look after the food requirements for their boarders.
Rooms are allotted to first-year students in the following manner:
For Boys - There are allotted 2 hostels, Aryabhatta hostel (PG hostel), and Hostel 3. In Aryabhatta Hostel, There are four subsections (East, West, North, and South). Each room is shared by 2 students.
For Girls - A separate Girls Hostel 1 (GH1) is allotted to first-year girls, on a three sharing basis.
You get the room right when you reach NIT Silchar (on the day of registrations i.e. July 22, 2019), as well as in the hostel warden’s office.
In-time for girls and boys hostel is 10 PM, before which they have to enter in their respective hostels except in case prior permission from the Hostel Coordinating Unit and Warden sir.

Rules for visitors

Parents are not allowed to stay in the hostels without prior permission from the Hostel Coordinating Unit. Female visitors to boys hostels and male visitors to girls hostel are not allowed. The Institute Guest House, flanked by green lawns and colorful horticulture remains a pleasant haven for the Institute Guests, whether from academia or alumni or parents of the students. The state-of-the-art Guest House with all modern infrastructures is one of its kind in the North East. Renowned personalities of the Nation, the likes of Former President of India and Bharat Ratna recipient, Late Shri APJ Abdul Kalam and Hon'ble Minister of Railways, Shri Suresh Prabhakar Prabhu are eminent guests who stayed in the Guest House during their visit to this Valley.


Basic Facalities in Hostels

Each room has a pair of beds, tables, chairs, ceiling fans, tube lights and is equipped with 24x7 high-speed LAN connectivity. There are 2 washrooms per 6 rooms with 24x7 running water (both hot and cold). Each Hostel has a mess (dining hall), a gym, a TV, and a facility for indoor games like Table Tennis, Carrom, Chess. Also, canteen shops are present in every Hostel.

Internet Facalities

In the Guest House, Administrative Building, Lecture Hall Complex, Residential Quarters, Health Centre, Estate Engg. Branch, SAC Building, NITS Cafe as well as the departments' WiFi is enabled. Just turn on your WiFi and connect to NITS-Wireless with proxy and password.
In the hostel rooms, LAN facilities are enabled, with a dedicated 1 Gbps leased line from Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd. (BSNL) which provides 24x7 Internet Connectivity to serve the Institute Internet needs. With this link, the Institute is getting around 400 to 600 Mbps bandwidth.
