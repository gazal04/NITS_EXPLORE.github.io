Transportation within NITS

1. Walking- Being the most primitive way of transportation, walking not only helps make memories of shared stories and laughter on the way but also helps maintain good health.
2. Cycling- Cycling is a quick, eco-friendly, and fun mode to get from place to place in the institute. It is a better mode than walking, as it helps reach the classroom faster while burning the calories on the way.
3. E-rickshaws- There are a few E-rickshaws meant to carry 4 passengers at a time, which frequent inside the campus. If you are lucky enough to find one with an available seat on time, don’t miss out on the rare opportunity XD As a fight against the COVID-19 pandemic, the institute has launched a special e-rickshaw to maintain social distancing.
4. Bus- The institute provides College buses for on-campus and pickup and dropdown outside the campus at specified timings on college days. The timings are modified during exam days, accordingly and often special services are given on occasions. .
