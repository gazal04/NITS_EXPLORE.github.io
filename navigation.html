Navigation

Spread over 625 acres, NIT Silchar offers you the most scenic atmosphere a student dreams of studying in. Located on the outskirts of the main town, NITS is surrounded by mesmerizing tea gardens and over 15 small lakes. The campus having a zero tolerance for plastic use, can ideally be acknowledged as a green campus with a plethora of vegetation and birds too.

HOW TO REACH CAMPUS

National Institute of Technology, Silchar is well connected to other parts of the country by a good network of road, rail, or air. The distance of Silchar from the economic hub of the state, Guwahati is only 326 km.

1. Airport
There are daily direct flights to Silchar from cities like Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Guwahati, and Imphal. Air India, Spice Jet, and Jet Airways operate flights from Silchar to Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Guwahati, and Imphal. From the airport, the Institute campus is approximately 35 Kms. Hired private taxi service is available from the airport at the price rate of Rs 800-900.

2. Railway Station
Silchar has a direct rail link with Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati. The campus is around 10 KMs from the Silchar railway station. Reserved auto-rickshaw is available for Rs 300- 350 on daily working days right outside the station gate.

3. Bus Station
Silchar is well connected with various cities through road. A 326 km long National Highway directly connects Silchar with Guwahati. A large number of day/night bus ply between Guwahati and Silchar in the morning and evening every day. The campus is more or less 13 km away from ISBT Silchar and daily commute is available very easily from the bus station.

MAP

The shortest route from the NITS campus to the main town takes nearly 30-40 minutes depending on the traffic and day timings. The ideal time for going to the main town would be 10-11 am and 4-5 pm during which traffic is minimal. The daily fares for auto-rickshaw are Rs 20-30 and you can find them lined up right outside the campus gate. Campus bus facilities are also available during morning and evening free of cost. The main town has all the amenities and daily necessities. Goldighi and Imperial malls are the best hang-out places for college students. Dominos, KFC, Baskin Robins, and what not; one can find all the delicacies over there. Famous clothing brands viz Pantaloons, Trends, Reliance, and grocer shops like Big Bazaar are located right inside the Goldighi Mall. There are several restaurants and food parlors for both vegetarian and non-vegetarian across the town. So, all in all, freshers need not worry about the unavailability of any essentials.
