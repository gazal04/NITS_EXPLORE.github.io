Basics

Spread over 625 acres, NIT Silchar offers you the most scenic atmosphere a student dreams of studying in. Located on the outskirts of the main town, NITS is surrounded by mesmerizing tea gardens and over 15 small lakes. The campus having a zero tolerance for plastic use, can ideally be acknowledged as a green campus with a plethora of vegetation and birds too.

Fair Price Shop (FPS)

Fair Price Shop, commonly referred to as FPS, is the one shop stop in NIT, Silchar. All the necessities of the students, starting from stationery to snacks, can be brought from FPS. Also, photocopies of documents, DTP, printouts of documents, etc can be done here as well. Be it the small getaways between the classes or the evening hangouts, FPS is also the mini food corner to satisfy our cravings.

Medical Facilities

All the students of NIT Silchar are entitled to free treatment at the Health Centre of the institute. Also, there are medical stores near the main gate of the institute, which is commonly referred to as the NIT point, where one can buy the necessary medicines. In addition to that, the Silchar Medical College & Hospital is just 3km away from the institute campus and hence, any major health issue can be attended to immediately.

Cell Phone Connection and Credit

Airtel, Reliance Jio and Vodafone connections are easily available at the Main Gate. Also, there are temporary stalls within the institute in the initial days of the session from which one can get a new sim connection too.

Banking Facilities

There is an SBI branch within the institute and all the transactions can be conveniently done here.

Fee Payment

DD in favour of Director, NIT, Silchar OR Through online payment SB Collect portal at State Bank of India (http://www.onlinesbi.com) SBI Collect > Accept > Assam > Educational Institute > Online Fee Collection Account NIT Silchar > Select Category and fill the necessary fields)

Part I

DD in favour of BHM, NIT Silchar, payable at Silchar OR Through the SB Collect portal at State Bank of India, to be notified by Dean SW. For foreign students: The tuition fee is: (a) USD 2,000 per semester for SAARC countries (b) USD 4,000 per semester for non-SAARC countries
